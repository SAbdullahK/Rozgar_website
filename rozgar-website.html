Our Story

Built in Karachi, for Karachi

Rozgar was born out of a simple frustration: finding a reliable plumber or electrician in Pakistan is harder than it should be. We're here to fix that.

The problem isn't that skilled workers don't exist — Pakistan has millions of talented kaarigar. The problem is trust, discovery, and fair payment.

Homeowners can't verify who's coming into their home. Workers can't build a reputation beyond word-of-mouth. Payments are cash-only and dispute-prone. Rozgar is the infrastructure layer this market has been missing.

Our Mission

Organise Pakistan's informal skilled workforce

We believe every Pakistani household deserves access to trustworthy, skilled professionals — and every skilled worker deserves a platform to earn fairly and build a career.

Rozgar is not just an app. It's a trust layer between millions of homes and the kaarigar who serve them. By bringing identity verification, transparent pricing, and in-app payments to an entirely informal market, we're building the foundation for a more organised, more equitable services economy.

Trust First

We don't onboard unverified workers. Every partner goes through identity and skills verification before their first job.

Fair for Both Sides

A platform that's good for customers but bad for workers isn't a platform worth building. We design for both.

Start Local, Scale Right

We're starting hyper-local in three Karachi neighbourhoods and getting the product right before expanding.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Rozgar, how it works, and when it's coming.

For Customers
What is Rozgar?
▾
Rozgar is a digital platform that connects Pakistani households with verified, skilled kaarigar — plumbers, electricians, carpenters, AC technicians, and more. Think of it as an Uber-style booking experience, but for home services, with full identity verification and in-app payment.
Is Rozgar available right now?
▾
Rozgar is currently in development and preparing for its launch in Karachi. We are targeting select areas — Defence, Clifton, and Gulshan-e-Iqbal — for our initial rollout. You can join the waitlist through our Contact page to be notified the moment we go live.
How do I book a service?
▾
Once launched, you will download the Rozgar customer app, describe your service need, set a budget or request quotes, and be matched with nearby verified workers. You can track the worker in real-time and pay securely through the app once the job is done to your satisfaction.
How are prices decided?
▾
Rozgar uses a transparent, negotiation-friendly model inspired by InDrive. You post a job with your budget, nearby workers respond with their offers, and you choose who you want. There are no hidden platform fees for customers — what you agree with the worker is what you pay.
What if I'm not happy with the work done?
▾
Every job on Rozgar comes with a post-job guarantee. If you're not satisfied, you can raise a dispute through the app before releasing payment. Our team reviews disputes and works to reach a fair resolution. We also encourage leaving honest ratings so other users can make informed decisions.
Is my payment safe?
▾
Yes. Payments on Rozgar are processed through a secure in-app wallet. You only release payment after the job is marked complete and you're satisfied. We do not share your financial details with workers, and all transactions are logged for your records.
Which areas does Rozgar serve?
▾
At launch, Rozgar will serve Defence Housing Authority (DHA), Clifton, and Gulshan-e-Iqbal in Karachi. We plan to expand to other Karachi areas and eventually other cities based on demand and worker supply.
For Workers (Kaarigar)
How do I join Rozgar as a worker?
▾
You will sign up through the Rozgar Partner app, submit your CNIC and a selfie for identity verification, and select the service categories you work in. Our team reviews your application and, once approved, you can start receiving job requests in your area.
Does Rozgar charge workers a fee?
▾
Rozgar operates on a commission-based model. A small platform commission is deducted from each completed job. There are no upfront registration fees. Workers keep the majority of what they earn, and all earnings are tracked transparently in the app.
When and how do workers get paid?
▾
Once a customer marks a job complete and approves payment, earnings are credited to your Rozgar wallet. You can withdraw to your bank account or mobile wallet (JazzCash/Easypaisa) on a regular schedule. All transactions are tracked in your dashboard.
Can I work on Rozgar part-time?
▾
Absolutely. Rozgar is designed for flexible work. You set your own availability and service radius. Accept jobs when you want, at the rates you're comfortable with. There's no minimum commitment required.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

AK Systems (operating as Rozgar) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways:

  • Information you provide: Name, phone number, email address, CNIC (for workers), and payment information during registration and use.
  • Information generated by use: Location data (when you use the app), job history, transaction records, ratings and reviews, and in-app communications.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support and improvement purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account on the platform.
  • To match customers with nearby verified workers using location data.
  • To process payments and maintain transaction records.
  • To verify worker identity and maintain platform safety.
  • To send service-related notifications and updates.
  • To improve our platform, detect fraud, and resolve disputes.

3. Location Data

Rozgar uses your device location to show you nearby workers and allow customers to track workers in transit to their job. Location data is only collected while you are actively using the app. You can revoke location permission through your device settings, though this will limit core platform functionality.

4. Worker CNIC and Identity Data

CNIC scans and selfie photos submitted by workers are used solely for identity verification purposes. This data is stored securely and is never shared with customers or third parties except where required by law.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the platform (cloud hosting, payment processing, SMS gateways).
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when required by Pakistani law.
  • Other users to the extent necessary for service delivery (e.g., sharing your name and rating with a matched worker).

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data storage, JWT-based authentication, and access controls. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to use strong passwords and report any suspected security issues to us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Transaction records may be retained longer for legal and financial compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us, subject to applicable legal requirements.

8. Children's Privacy

Rozgar is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a minor has registered, we will promptly delete their account and data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by Rozgar. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rozgar.pk. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Rozgar after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, email privacy@rozgar.pk or visit our Contact page.
